Useful Plants

‘Useful plants’ is a small resource built to present functional plants, that are easy to obtain, grow and care for. Some of those presented are best for gardens, other for indoors.

All plants are presented with their photo and common name and Latin name equally. The names link directly to relevant wikipedia page, containing all the necessary information.

The icons below each name describe their preferences:

abundant in water
moderate watering
rare watering

fully sunny site
partially sunny site
shade

Additionally, two icons indicate if the plant is resistant to certain temperatures:

resistant to heat
resistant to cold

Of course, information about plant’s resistance shouldn’t be treated with common sense.
