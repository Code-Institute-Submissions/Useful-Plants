Useful Plants

Plants you find below are beneficial for bees and other pollinating insects. They are useful both when you own bees and when you want to attract and help wild bees survive. If you choose the latter, you can also put in your garden/balcony wild bees hotel.

For bees

Lavender
(Lavandula)
Sage
(Salvia officinalis)
Forget-me-not
(Myosotis sylvatica)

(Aster)

(Rosmarinus officinalis)

(Mentha)
